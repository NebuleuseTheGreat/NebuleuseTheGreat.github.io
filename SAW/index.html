Website create by Nébuleuse

what is SAW ?

It's a mod in Stablast's modding space, with an original new shiptree, and other interesting features like asteroid waves.

3 teams

As with the classic team mode, SAW also has 3 stations, the only difference being that here the basic colors don't change.

new shiptree

SAW features a large, fully-customized Shiptree, with lots of different ships and play styles.

asteroid wave

The main feature of the SAW mod is that, at certain times, powerful asteroid waves are triggered, forcing each team to adapt to this new constraint and enabling new strategies.

other added

There are also other special features, such as random sector names in a new, unique list and a slightly larger map.

Dev team

Here are the 4 creators of the SAW mod. You'll find a short description of who they are, and their networks.

Megalodon

Co-Founder, mod developer

I've been playing Starblast since 2017, I'm one of the best European players, creator of the famous "Megs dueling" mod, Alien Speedrun, as well as lead developer of Starblast Asteroid War.

Hazard

Co-Founder, Shiptree builder, rendering designer

I started this game in March 2017, but remained anonymous until 2018. Then I looked for balance and competition in the game, lately I'm focusing more on creating, developing or redesigning mods than on the game. I try to do a few events and mods for the community.

Nébuleuse

Co-Founder, organizer, rendering designer

I started playing Starblast in 2017, I consider myself a pretty good player especially in team mode, I always stay active on the game and in the community, in SAW I took care of the community part, I greatly helped its creation, so do this site.

LX

Founder, organizer, rendering designer

I've been playing starblast since 2018, I'm the founder of SAW mod and we worked for 1 year to develop and balance the best mod. The idea for SAW came from me, and I'm the graphic designer for this project.
